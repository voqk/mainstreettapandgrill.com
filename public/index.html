    Main Street Tap and Grill #

    The purpose of this website is to warn the public about Main Street Tap & Grill (MST&G), a restaurant and bar in Houston, TX. MST&G regularly overserves their patrons. More people will be hurt or killed until TABC or the City of Houston takes action and shuts them down. MST&G is a danger to the public.
